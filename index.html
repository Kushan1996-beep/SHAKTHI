About Our School

Our History

Established in 2003, Shakthi Pre School has been serving the community of TRACT 04, RAJANGANAYA for over 20 years. With a rich history of academic excellence, we have educated over 1500 students who have gone on to succeed in various fields.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where children can develop their full potential. We aim to instill a love for learning that will last a lifetime.

Our Approach

At Shakthi Pre School, we follow a child-centered approach to education. Our curriculum is designed to promote holistic development through play-based learning, creative activities, and age-appropriate academic instruction.
